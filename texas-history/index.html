Texas History: Interactive Apps & Games

Explore all eight units of 7th grade Texas History (TEKS §113.19) through click-and-explore apps and story-driven games. Interactive apps are free to use. Games are free for students to play — a class membership unlocks the Teacher Command Center with live results and downloadable reports.

Games require membership for teacher tools  •  Apps are always open
